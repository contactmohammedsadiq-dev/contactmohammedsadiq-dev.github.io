Assalamu Alaikum

At Al-Hidayah Madrasa, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive Islamic education rooted in the Quran and Sunnah. Our mission is to nurture the minds and hearts of our students, helping them develop a strong foundation in their faith while preparing them to be righteous, knowledgeable, and productive members of society.

We offer a structured curriculum that includes Quran memorization (Hifz), Islamic studies, Arabic language, and moral character development. Our experienced teachers are committed to creating a nurturing environment where students can thrive spiritually and academically.
