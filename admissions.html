Admission Requirements

General Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate or ID
  • Recent passport-sized photograph
  • Parent/Guardian contact information
  • Emergency contact details

Program-Specific Requirements

  • Hifz Program: Basic Quran reading ability
  • Islamic Studies: Age-appropriate placement
  • Arabic Language: Placement test (if applicable)
  • Children's Program: Parent consent form

Commitment Expected

  • Regular attendance (minimum 80%)
  • Respectful behavior and Islamic etiquette
  • Completion of assignments and homework
  • Active participation in class
  • Adherence to madrasa policies

Enrollment Process

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the online registration form below or visit our office to fill out a paper application.

2

Initial Review

Our admissions team will review your application and contact you within 3-5 business days.

3

Assessment/Interview

Schedule an assessment (for Hifz/Arabic) or parent-student interview to discuss program details and expectations.

4

Registration & Payment

Upon acceptance, complete registration by submitting required documents and paying the registration fee.

5

Begin Classes

Receive your class schedule and start your Islamic education journey with us!

Fee Structure

We strive to make Islamic education accessible to all. Our fees are kept reasonable, and financial assistance is available for those in need. No student is turned away due to financial constraints - please speak with our administration in confidence.

Program Registration Fee Monthly Fee Annual Fee
Quran Memorization (Hifz) $50 $150 $1,500
Islamic Studies (Full Program) $50 $100 $1,000
Arabic Language (Per Level) $50 $120 $1,200
Children's Weekend Program $30 $75 $750
Youth Leadership Program $30 $60 $600
Adult Study Circles $25 $50 $500

Important Notes:

  • Registration fee is one-time per academic year and non-refundable
  • Annual payment option includes a 10% discount
  • Sibling discounts available: 10% for second child, 15% for third and subsequent
  • Payment plans available upon request
  • Financial assistance and scholarships available - please inquire confidentially
  • Fees include all course materials and textbooks
