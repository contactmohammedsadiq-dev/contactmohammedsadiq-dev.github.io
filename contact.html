Directions

By Car: The madrasa is easily accessible from Highway 123. Take Exit 45 and turn right onto Islamic Center Road. We are located on the left side, approximately 2 miles from the exit.

By Public Transport: Take Bus Route 15 or 22 and get off at the "Islamic Center" stop. The madrasa is a short 5-minute walk from the bus stop.

Parking: Free parking is available on-site for visitors and students.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your office hours?

Our office is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

How can I schedule a visit?

You can schedule a visit by calling us at +1 (555) 123-4567 or sending an email to info@alhidayahmadrasa.me. We welcome prospective students and their families to tour our facility.

Do you offer online classes?

Currently, most of our programs are conducted in-person. However, we do offer some online options for specific courses. Please contact us to learn more about online availability.

Is financial assistance available?

Yes, we offer financial assistance to families in need. All requests are handled confidentially. Please speak with our admissions office to discuss available options.

How do I register for a program?

You can register online through our Admissions page, visit our office in person, or call us to request a registration form.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention. Our student-teacher ratio varies by program but typically ranges from 5:1 to 15:1.
