Quran Memorization (Hifz Program)

Our Hifz program is designed to help students memorize the entire Quran with proper Tajweed under the guidance of certified and experienced instructors who hold Ijazah in Quranic recitation.

Program Features:

  • One-on-one sessions with qualified Quran teachers
  • Structured memorization methodology proven over centuries
  • Regular revision (Muraja'ah) schedules
  • Tajweed correction and improvement
  • Monthly progress assessments
  • Supportive learning environment

Age Groups:

Children (7-12 years)

Foundation level with emphasis on proper pronunciation and gradual memorization

Youth (13-18 years)

Intensive Hifz program with advanced Tajweed and understanding

Mon-Fri: 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Flexible schedule accommodating working professionals

Islamic Studies

Comprehensive Islamic education covering essential knowledge every Muslim needs. Our curriculum is based on authentic sources and classical Islamic scholarship.

Core Subjects:

Aqeedah (Belief)

Understanding Islamic creed, pillars of faith, and correct beliefs

Fiqh (Jurisprudence)

Islamic law, worship practices, and daily life rulings

Seerah (Biography)

Life of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) and his companions

Hadith Studies

Study of Prophetic traditions and their application

Tafsir (Quran Exegesis)

Understanding and interpretation of the Quran

Islamic History

Muslim civilization, caliphates, and historical events

Class Schedules:

  • Weekend Program: Saturdays & Sundays, 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Evening Program: Monday - Thursday, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
  • Intensive Summer Program: June - August, Mon-Fri, 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Arabic Language Program

Master the Arabic language - the language of the Quran - through our structured courses designed for non-native speakers at all proficiency levels.

Program Levels:

Level 1 - Beginner

Duration: 6 months

  • Arabic alphabet and pronunciation
  • Basic vocabulary and grammar
  • Simple conversations
  • Reading short sentences

Level 2 - Intermediate

Duration: 8 months

  • Expanded vocabulary
  • Grammar and sentence structure
  • Reading comprehension
  • Writing skills development

Level 3 - Advanced

Duration: 10 months

  • Classical Arabic grammar (Nahw & Sarf)
  • Quranic Arabic
  • Literary texts
  • Advanced composition

Class Times:

Multiple time slots available:

  • Morning Classes: Tuesday & Thursday, 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Evening Classes: Monday & Wednesday, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Weekend Classes: Sundays, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
